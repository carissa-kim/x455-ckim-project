I used Adobe InDesign to create this magazine. I focused on utilizing grids to create a well-balanced, easy to read layout. I strategically added dark and light elements to accentuate small ideas without leaving the reader drowning in text.
The body copy is meant to be large and round for quick reading and is accented by subtle script and large block type to add flavor and personality.
