Project 3

Advertising Campaign


The goal of this project was to take different paintings, select a particular product and create a poster that incorporates the product seamlessly as part of an advertisement.

Taking something as simple and recognizable as a Starbucks cup, I edited contrast, brightness, and texture in order to integrate the cup into various paintings. Each painting has a different brush style and needs a matching styled cup. I worked with Adobe photoshop to mask the cup and add it to the paintings as if they were supposed to be there.
