Project 2

Multiplatform Marketing


The project started with a poster design.
I emphasized the duality, the ominousness, and the wonder portrayed in the film.

Coraline Film Trailer

Inspiration

The official trailer of Coraline from Laika studios. I pulled themes from the movie and focused on the duality in the story. If you know the story, I tied key aspects together to create a complete picture of the movie. If you are unfamiliar with the story, I attempted to convey an ominousness that would intrigue viewers and a lightness that would not deter younger audiences.
