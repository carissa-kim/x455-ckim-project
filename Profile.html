Hello & Welcome

I'm Carissa Kim

  • Professional Program in Graphic Design, UC Berkeley
  • Master's of Science, Microbiology & Immunology
  • Bachelor's of Science, Biology

I'm Carissa Kim. Born and raised on Oahu, HI. Greatly influence by asian culture.


I have been exposed to a variety of cultures and traditions. Eclectic designs and traditional motifs are abundant in Hawaii. I want to create designs that show the multi-personality of the client, combining traits that emphasize a clean, crisp, yet eye-catching design.



7 years as a laboratory research scientist.


I bring a methodical and analytical approach to designing. I want my designs to emphasize a balance of light and dark, large and small, straight and curved.



Combining my love of art with my knack for science.


I love experimenting with new styles and learning interesting techniques. I love meticulously recreating designs and creating new ones with my own flair. I am well-versed in digital design softwares such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe XD, and Adobe Photoshop.
One of my goals is to create designs, particularly scientific ones, that are enjoyed and appreciated by many.
